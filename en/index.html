What is Fixground?

Fixground is a quiet circle for people who notice problems and try to improve them.

We don’t stop at ideas. We build, test, and document small attempts.

Principles

We value work over titles.
We improve systems, not assign blame.
We prefer small attempts over perfect plans.
We respect others’ time and effort.
We validate together rather than judge.

For / Not for

For

  • People who have tried to improve at least one thing they noticed
  • People driven by solving, regardless of role
  • People comfortable with quiet building and sharing

Not for

  • People primarily seeking promotion or personal branding
  • People who only collect ideas without execution
  • People who center conversations on cynicism and blame

How to Connect
